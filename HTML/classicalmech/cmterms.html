Physical Properties

Velocity
Time rate of change of position of object
Momentum
Product of mass and velocity
Kinetic Energy
Energy of motion
Potential Energy
Energy due to a conservative force field

Physical Bodies

Particle
Object with mass, but insignificant size and no internal degrees of freedom. When working with particles, we formulate equations using the constraint volume = 0 and length = 0.

Laws of Motion

Galilean Invariance
The laws of motion (importantly, acceleration) remain the same in all inertial reference frames
Conservation of Momentum
Momentum is always conserved
Work-Energy Theorem
Relation between work done and energy
