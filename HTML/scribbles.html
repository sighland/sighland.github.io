The magnetic force induced by a solenoid on a magnetic object inside it will cause all the dipole moments in the object to align in same direction.
For paramagnetic objects, this is in direction of B field.
For diamagnetic objects, this is opposite it.
Either way, putting a magnetic material in a solenoid? That's an inductor!
The magnetic force of a charge is 0 if the direction of motion is the same as the direction of the magnetic field
